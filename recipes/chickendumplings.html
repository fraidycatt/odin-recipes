Easy Chicken & Dumplings

A southern weeknight dinner, utilizing rotisserie chicken for speed and deliciousness.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 rotisserie chicken
  • 2 cartons of chicken broth
  • 1 can of cream of chicken
  • Flour
  • A box of biscuit mix-- bisquick is fine
  • salt & pepper

STEPS

  1. Bring your chicken broth to a simmer in a large pot. Add cream of chicken and mix well.
  2. While it's getting hot, shred your rotisserie chicken. Add to the pot with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Mix up the biscuits according to package instructions. Scoop about a tablespoon of mix at a time into a separate bowl of flour, coat the dumpling in it, and place gently into the simmering pot of broth and chicken. You may not use all of the mix.
  4. Simmer until the biscuits are done and the soup thickens. Serve hot.
