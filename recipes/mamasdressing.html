Mama's Dressing

A Thanksgiving classic from my childhood.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 rotisserie chicken, shredded, juices set aside
  • 2 bags of cotton country cornbread mix
  • 1 box of jiffy cornbread mix
  • 1 can cream of chicken
  • 1 can cream of celery
  • 1 carton chicken broth
  • ground sage
  • boiled, diced eggs

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Bake cornbread according to package instructions and let cool.
  3. Crumble cornbread together into a casserole pan. Incorporate a can of cream of chicken, about a half can of cream of celery, and chicken broth. The mixture should be very moist, as a lot absorbs into the cornbread during baking.
  4. Mix in the diced eggs, sage, and other seasonings to taste.
  5. Bake in the oven for about an hour.
